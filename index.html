The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker

One of the very first games I played growing up was the Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker in the early 2000s. My siblings and I got a Nintendo GameCube as a Christmas gift, and it came with the Legend of Zelda: Collectors Disc. Prior to this, we only really got to play video games when our cousins would come visit during the Thanksgiving holiday.

While the collectors disc only came with a 20-minute demo version of The Wind Waker game, my sister and I would take turns trying to progress as far as possible in the demo.
